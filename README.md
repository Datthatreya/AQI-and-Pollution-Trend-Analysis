## 🚀 Overview
This project analyzes **air pollution trends across states and regions**, evaluating AQI levels, pollutant distribution, health impact, and EV adoption to identify patterns and support **data-driven environmental strategies**.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics
- 🌫️ Average AQI: 118.85  
- 🗺️ States Covered: 32  
- 📡 Monitoring Stations: 777K+  
- 📊 Maximum AQI: 500  
- 📉 Minimum AQI: 3  

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
- 🚨 Cities like **Byrnihat, Begusarai, Delhi** consistently show highest AQI levels  
- 🌿 Cleanest regions include **Tirunelveli, Madikeri, Thanjavur**  
- 📊 Majority of days fall under **Moderate and Satisfactory AQI categories**  
- ⚠️ Severe AQI days are fewer but indicate critical pollution spikes  
- 🧪 **PM10 and PM2.5** are the dominant pollutants across regions  
- 🏥 Pollution-related diseases (Diarrheal, Food Poisoning) show significant case counts  
- 👶 Elderly (65+) and children (0–4) are most affected age groups  

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions Answered
- Which regions consistently experience high pollution levels?  
- What are the most common pollutant types across states?  
- How does AQI vary across population-heavy regions?  
- Which age groups are most impacted by air pollution?  
- Is EV adoption helping reduce AQI levels?

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- 📌 KPI cards (AQI, states, monitoring stations)  
- 📉 AQI trend analysis (monthly/yearly)  
- 🏆 Top 5 polluted and cleanest areas  
- 📊 AQI category distribution (Good → Severe)  
- 🧪 Pollutant distribution analysis  
- 🏥 Health impact analysis (cases & deaths)  
- 👥 Population vs AQI comparison  
- 🚗 EV adoption analysis by state and category  
- 📅 Weekday vs Weekend AQI comparison  
- 🎛️ Interactive slicers (Year, Month, State, Area)
